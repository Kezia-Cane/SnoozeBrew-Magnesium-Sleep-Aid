General

What is SnoozeBrew?

SnoozeBrew is a melatonin-free nightly cocoa made to support a calmer wind-down, deeper sleep, and clearer mornings. The formula story centers on magnesium glycinate, L-theanine, valerian root, 5-HTP, and passionflower.

Is SnoozeBrew melatonin-free?

Yes. SnoozeBrew is positioned as melatonin-free, with the goal of supporting sleep without that heavy next-morning feeling many people want to avoid.

How is SnoozeBrew different from typical sleep products?

The product is built around a warm nightly ritual instead of a quick pill or gummy moment. It also focuses on melatonin-free support, so the experience is designed to feel steadier and more routine-friendly.

Who is SnoozeBrew designed for?

SnoozeBrew is designed for adults who want calmer evenings, more restorative sleep, and clearer mornings without leaning on melatonin-heavy formulas.

How soon might I notice a difference?

Results can vary, but the brand positioning is built around consistent nightly use. Many people look for a smoother wind-down first, with the bedtime ritual becoming more noticeable as it turns into a habit.

How do I know I’m ordering authentic SnoozeBrew?

The safest path is to order directly through SnoozeBrew’s official site flow and support channels. If you are unsure about a link, contact hello@trysnoozebrew.com before placing an order.

Usage

How do I use SnoozeBrew?

Mix one scoop into warm water, milk, or your favorite plant-based alternative and sip it as part of your evening routine.

When should I drink it?

The guidance on the product page recommends drinking it about 30 minutes before bed so your body has time to settle into a calmer nighttime rhythm.

Do I need to use it every night?

SnoozeBrew is framed as a nightly ritual, so consistency is the intended experience. One jar contains 30 servings, which lines up with once-nightly use for about a month.

What can I mix it with?

You can mix it with warm water, dairy milk, or a plant-based milk alternative depending on how rich or light you want the ritual to feel.

How many servings are in each jar?

Each jar contains 30 servings, which is about one month of use if you enjoy one scoop nightly.

Can SnoozeBrew fit into my existing bedtime routine?

Yes. It is designed to feel like the warm final step in a wind-down routine, so it can sit alongside reading, stretching, journaling, or other calming evening habits.

Ingredients & Formula

What ingredients are inside each scoop?

The supplement facts panel calls out valerian root extract, L-theanine, magnesium glycinate, 5-HTP, passionflower extract, and sodium as sodium citrate.

Why magnesium glycinate?

SnoozeBrew highlights magnesium glycinate because it is a gentle, bioavailable magnesium form that supports nightly relaxation without the harsher digestive reputation some other forms can have.

What do the botanicals in the formula do?

The formula story uses valerian root and passionflower to support a softer wind-down, while L-theanine and 5-HTP are positioned to help the body and mind ease into rest.

Is the formula vegan, gluten-free, and sugar-free?

Yes. The product page positions SnoozeBrew as melatonin-free, vegan, gluten-free, and made with 0g total sugar per serving.

Will SnoozeBrew make me feel groggy the next morning?

That is the opposite of what the formula is built for. SnoozeBrew is positioned around clearer mornings rather than a heavy, foggy wake-up.

Where can I see the full ingredient information?

You can review the supplement facts and ingredient story directly on the main product page, including the amounts listed for the core active ingredients.
