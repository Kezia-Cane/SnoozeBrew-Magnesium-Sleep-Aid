Privacy Policy

Effective Date:May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Snooze Brew collects, how we use it, and what choices you have when interacting with our website, emails, and purchases.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, shipping address, billing details, and order history.

We may also collect technical data such as browser type, device information, pages visited, referral sources, and cookie-based session activity.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to process orders, communicate about shipments, manage subscriptions, improve the website experience, and respond to customer support requests.

We may also use your information for internal analytics, fraud prevention, and operational improvements.

3. Email & Marketing

If you opt in, we may send product updates, bedtime tips, and promotional offers.

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in the message.

4. Sharing Information

We may share information with service providers that help us run the business, such as payment processors, email platforms, analytics tools, and shipping carriers.

We do not sell your personal information in the ordinary course of running Snooze Brew.

5. Cookies & Site Tools

Like many ecommerce sites, we use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure performance, and understand how visitors use the site.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings, though disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to help protect personal information, but no website or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

7. Your Choices

You may contact us to request updates or deletion of certain personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements.

If you are a U.S. resident with additional privacy rights under applicable state law, we will review requests in accordance with those laws.

Privacy Questions?

Email privacy@trySnooze Brew.com if you have questions about how your information is used.
