Frequently Asked Questions

What is Snooze Brew designed to support?

Snooze Brew is designed to support a calmer wind-down, deeper sleep, and clearer mornings through a simple nightly ritual.

When should I drink it?

Most people mix one scoop about 30 minutes before bed so the routine can ease them into a calmer nighttime rhythm.
